Exoplanets (Click me)

Exoplanets are planets located outside of our solar system!

Exoplanets consist of some of the most weirdest experiences regarding weather, huge lava planets, mass, minerals, potential New Super Earths and places which can harbour life.

Stars

Stars are hot balls of gas undergoing nuclear reactions in order to produce the light they emit. There are several types of stars, such as: Neutron Stars, Yellow Dward Stars(Our Sun), Red Dwarf and Blue Dwarfs.
